Item Specifics
Theme : baseballProduct Type: Postcard
Satchel Paige was one of the early great black players in baseball , first in the Negro League and then in the Major Leagues at the end of his career. He was elected to the Hall of Fame in 1971. He pitched for the great Monarchs in the Negro league from 1939-42.

The item for sale is a rare exhibit photo card of Satchel that measures 3.375 " by 5.375 ". Based on the cards in the collection that I purchased the card is most likely from 1947 when he pitched for the Cleveland Indians. The card is in fair condition . It has slight scratches on the surface and and old pieces of scotch tape at top and bottom where it was attached to a scrap book.The card is still a rare and a great collectible. The back of the card is clean. Please review the picture carefully since the card is being sold as is.
